                     The Bioscience Challenge
                

                

                    

                        In Malaysian oil palm milling and plantation management, harvesting fresh fruit bunches (FFB) at the precise biological maturity peak is critical. Harvesting too early results in under-developed oil content in the mesocarp (pulp), while harvesting too late leads to over-ripe fruits dropping, soaring Free Fatty Acids (FFA) which lowers quality, and manual collection losses.
                    

                    

                        Biological studies show that oil accumulation in the developing mesocarp follows a sigmoidal (logistic) curve over Weeks After Anthesis (WAA). 
                    

                    

                        The derivative f'(x) represents the instantaneous oil synthesis rate (change in oil percentage per week). Identifying this rate helps agricultural scientists pinpoint the exact transition phases between cell division, rapid oil storage, and maturation.
                    

                

            


            
            

                

                     The Mathematical Model
                

                

                    

                        
Accumulation Function: f(x)

                        

                            $$f(x) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(x - x_0)}}$$
                        

                        

                            Where L is max oil content ($55\%$ dry weight), k is accumulation rate constant ($0.5$), and x₀ is the inflection point ($16$ WAA).
                        

                    


                    

                        
First Derivative: f'(x) [Rate of Change]

                        

                            $$f'(x) = \frac{L \cdot k \cdot e^{-k(x - x_0)}}{(1 + e^{-k(x - x_0)})^2} = k \cdot f(x) \left(1 - \frac{f(x)}{L}\right)$$
                        

                        

                            Represents the speed of lipid synthesis inside mesocarp cell vacuoles per week.
